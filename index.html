Maelekezo

Jinsi ya kufunga app baada ya kupakua
Android
01
Bofya faili la APK ulilolipakua Linaonekana kwenye "Downloads" ya simu yako.
02
Onyo la "Unknown source" Bofya "Settings" kwenye ujumbe huo, geuza "Allow from this source" kuwa On.
03
Bofya "Install" tena App itafungwa ndani ya sekunde chache.
Windows
01
Fungua faili la .zip ulilolipakua Bofya "Extract All" kupata folder ya app.
02
Onyo la "Windows protected your PC" Bofya "More info", kisha "Run anyway" — hii ni kawaida kwa apps bila Microsoft Store license.
03
Fungua faili la .exe ndani ya folder App itafunguka moja kwa moja, hauhitaji "install" ya ziada.
macOS
01
Fungua faili la .dmg ulilolipakua Dirisha jipya litafunguka likiwa na icon ya app.
02
Vuta icon kwenda "Applications" Kisha funga dirisha hilo.
03
Onyo la "Apple cannot verify" Fungua "System Settings → Privacy & Security", bofya "Open Anyway" karibu na jina la app.
